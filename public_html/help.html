Geometric Card Game

Game play

This is a turn-by-turn game, each turn you can do one of the four actions: drawing a card, discarding a card, merging two cards, and playing a card.

Once the action is done, you need to click "End turn" button to end your turn.

Controls

There are four buttons on the bottom of the screen:

Draw Merge Discard Play

You need to first click/touch a card, it will be selected, and then you can click the "Discard" or "Play" button.

Or you can select two cards, and click "Merge" button to merge them into an advanced card.

Or if you have no cards on hand, click "Draw" to gain a new basic card.
